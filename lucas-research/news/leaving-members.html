Two Members Leaves LUCAS Group

On 12th of July LUCAS group hosted a group lunch to wish all the best to two leaving members of our group: Lecturer Dr Hyondong Oh and Technician Andy Pickering.

Andy Pickering is leaving to become an Electronic Test Engineer in Brush Traction, Loughborough.

Dr Hyondong Oh is leaving to become an Assistant Professor in School of Mechanical and Nuclear Engineering, UNIST (Ulsan National Institute of Science and Technology), South Korea.
