Dr Matthew Coombes attend ICUAS (2017) conference

Group member Dr. Matthew Coombes attended ICUAS (2017) held on Jun3 13-16, Miami, USA. 

He co-chaired the session Path Planning-V and delivered his presentation titled "Boustrophedon Coverage Path Planning for UAV Aerial Surveys in Wind ".
